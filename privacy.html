Introduction

Simple Fast Translation is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information.

Information Collection

Simple Fast Translation does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app operates completely offline and does not require internet access permissions.

All translation operations are performed locally on your device. We do not store or process any of your text or translation data on external servers.

App Permissions

The app only requires minimal permissions necessary for basic functionality. We do not request network access or any permissions that could compromise your privacy.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
